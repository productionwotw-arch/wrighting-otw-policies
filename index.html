Wrighting OTW

Wrighting OTW is a personal content-publishing tool. It authenticates to its operator's own TikTok and Instagram accounts and publishes short-form video content that the operator writes and produces themselves.

It is not a public product, does not accept other users, and does not access or store data belonging to anyone other than its operator.
