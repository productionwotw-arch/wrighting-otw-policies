Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-20

This privacy policy covers the content-publishing application ("the App") used to automate posting to TikTok and Instagram on behalf of its own operator's accounts.

Who this covers

The App is operated by a single individual for personal content-publishing purposes. It is not a public product and does not offer service to third-party users.

What data the App accesses

The App uses TikTok's Content Posting API and Instagram's Graph API, via OAuth, solely to:

  • Authenticate the operator's own TikTok and Instagram accounts.
  • Upload and publish video content that the operator created, to the operator's own accounts.

The App does not access, read, or store any data belonging to other users of TikTok or Instagram. It does not collect analytics on viewers, followers, or any third party.
